Meet Your 2016 Co-Chairs
Heather VanKeuren and Steve Esmacher. Many of you may know Heather best as the leader of the route safety motorcycle crew and from her famous skunk helmet. Heather has been participating in Ride for the Feast since 2008. Not only is she a member of the Ride for the Feast committee, she is also a Moveable Feast Board Member and a member of the Dining Out For Life committee. This will be Heather's second year as the co-chair of the committee.

Committee Help
Are you a rider or volunteer that's interested in getting more involved with Ride for the Feast? We're looking for dedicated riders and crew to join our Ride for the Feast committee!

Our goal is to have representation from each Ride for the Feast team present on the committee, so if you're team is not yet represented, we urge you to nominate a team member to join.

Even if your team is already represented we'd still love your help!

For more information about committee roles, responsibilities and time commitments please contact Sam at sflottemesch@mfeast.org or 410-327-3420 x. 11.
